Program Overview

Product Design Foundation: Igniting Creative Prowess

Our Product Design Foundation program is your gateway to the exciting realm of product design. Tailored for early professionals with a passion for design, this program delivers a foundational understanding of the art and science of product design. Through a condensed yet comprehensive curriculum, you’ll embark on a transformative journey, honing your design skills, mastering user-centric thinking, and acquiring the proficiency to craft captivating user experiences.

By the program’s end, you’ll possess a solid grasp of product design essentials, empowering you to wield design tools, employ user-centric methodologies, and launch your own design projects. This program fuels your creativity and equips you with the skills to make a lasting impact in the dynamic world of design.

  • Product Knowledge

  • Core Design Principles

  • Design Thinking

  • User Centric Thinking

  • Creativity and Innovation

  • Product Prototyping

  • Industry Insights

  • Critical Thinking

  • Portfolio Development

  • Proficiency in design tools

  • Relevant Softskills

  • Project Execution

How You Will Learn

Methods: Virtual and Physical classes, Business case-studies, Virtual projects, and Interactive quizzes.

Virtual Classes

Our virtual classes are led by industry experts who have years of experience in product management. Through our virtual classes, you will have the opportunity to interact with experienced product management professionals and gain a deep understanding of the latest industry tools and techniques. You will also be able to connect with other aspiring product managers, share experiences and learn from each other.

Hands-On Projects

Apply your learning through hands-on projects, tackling design challenges, and building a professional design portfolio.

Peer Collaboration

Collaborate with fellow learners, sharing ideas, providing feedback, and working together on team projects.

Expert Guidance

Benefit from the wisdom of experienced design instructors who offer mentorship, guidance, and insights.

Real-World Scenarios

Solve practical design problems, simulating real-world scenarios to enhance your problem-solving skills.

Continuous Evaluation

Receive feedback on your work, ensuring your progress and development throughout the program.
