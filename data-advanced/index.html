Program Overview

The Data Foundations course for Advanced Training is a comprehensive program designed for individuals seeking advanced skills and knowledge in data analytics.

This course covers four major topics: Microsoft Excel, Power BI, SQL, and Tableau. Participants will gain in-depth knowledge of each tool and its application in data analysis, as well as real-world projects and portfolio development to showcase their proficiency in data analytics.

  • Advanced SQL Functions

  • Designing Relational Databases

  • Data Cleaning

  • Creating Complex Queries

  • Building Complex Formulas

  • Advanced Data Analysis

  • Optimizing Database Performance

  • Collaborating With Others

  • Visualizing Data Effectively

  • Handling Advance Data Types

Who Should Attend

This course is ideal for individuals seeking to advance their career in data analytics, including data analysts, business analysts, financial analysts, and anyone who wants to learn advanced data analytics skills. Prior experience in data analytics is recommended, but not required.

How You Will Learn

Methods: Virtual and Physical classes, Business case-studies, Virtual projects, and Interactive quizzes.

Virtual Classes

You will participate in live virtual classes that will provide a platform for discussions, question and answer sessions, and feedback. The virtual classes are designed to be interactive, with students encouraged to ask questions and participate in group discussions.

Business Case Studies

The course will utilize real-life business case studies that demonstrate how data analysis can be used in decision-making. The case studies will provide you with practical examples of data analysis application in different industries, including marketing, finance, and healthcare.

Virtual Projects

You will engage in virtual projects that will allow you to apply the concepts you have learnt. The projects will involve analyzing data, creating visualizations, and drawing insights from the data. You will receive feedback on your work from instructors, providing you with valuable insights on their progress.

Interactive Quizzes

The program includes interactive quizzes that will test your understanding of the concepts and tools covered in each module. The quizzes are designed to be engaging and will provide instant feedback on the student's performance.

Overall, the course is designed to provide a mix of theoretical concepts and practical applications to ensure you acquire a holistic understanding of data analysis.

What You Will Learn

I. Microsoft Excel for Data Analysis

  • Gain an in-depth understanding of Excel for data analysis, including Pivot Tables, Hlookup, Vlookup, and Exact Match functions.
  • Learn how to create dynamic dashboards that display and visualize data in real-time.
  • Acquire advanced skills in Excel formulas and functions for data analysis, such as array formulas, index match, and conditional formatting.
  • Master Excel’s data visualization tools, including charts, graphs, and sparklines.

ll. Power BI for Data Modeling and Visualization

  • Learn how to design and create effective data models for efficient data analysis.
  • Master Data Analysis Expressions (DAX) for complex calculations, such as time intelligence, filtering, and ranking.
  • Acquire advanced skills in Power BI’s visualization tools, including custom visuals, drill-through, and drill-down.
  • Gain experience in designing and creating reports, dashboards, and scorecards for data-driven decision-making.

lll. SQL for Data Analysis

  • Understand the fundamentals of SQL and relational databases, including tables, keys, and constraints.
  • Learn how to write advanced queries using aggregate functions, subqueries, and common table expressions (CTEs).
  • Acquire skills in using SQL for data manipulation, such as updates, inserts, and deletes.
  • Gain experience in optimizing SQL queries for performance and scalability.

IV. Tableau for Data Visualization and Storytelling

  • Master the art of storytelling in data visualization, including design principles, visual hierarchy, and narrative flow.
  • Acquire advanced skills in Tableau’s visualization tools, such as data blending, dual-axis charts, and set analysis.
  • Learn how to design and create interactive dashboards that engage and inform users.
  • Gain experience in building complex calculations, such as table calculations, LOD expressions, and parameter controls.

V. Real-World Projects and Portfolio Development

  • Master the art of storytelling in data visualization, including design principles, visual hierarchy, and narrative flow.
  • Acquire advanced skills in Tableau’s visualization tools, such as data blending, dual-axis charts, and set analysis.
  • Learn how to design and create interactive dashboards that engage and inform users.
  • Gain experience in building complex calculations, such as table calculations, LOD expressions, and parameter controls.

    By the end of the Data Foundations course for Advanced Training, you will have gained advanced skills and knowledge in data analytics using Excel, Power BI, SQL, and Tableau. You will have developed a portfolio showcasing your proficiency in data analysis and completed real-world projects that demonstrate your expertise. You will also have built a network of professionals in the field and expanded your career opportunities in the data analytics industry. Enroll now to take your data analytics skills to the next level!

Class Requirements

  • 4 weeks, 11-12 hours/week

  • Access to a laptop or desktop computer with a minimum of 4gig ram, and 256g SSD or 500g HDD Rom.

  • Access to a stable internet connection

  • Proficiency in written and spoken English
